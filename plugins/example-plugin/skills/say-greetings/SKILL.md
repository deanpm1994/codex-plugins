# say-greetings

This skill allows the bot to greet users in a friendly manner.
User messages that contain greetings will trigger the bot to respond with a friendly greeting.

-- **Usage**: When a user sends a greeting message (e.g., "Hello", "Hi", "Good morning"), the bot will respond with a friendly greeting.

## Culture and Language Support

This skill supports multiple cultures and languages, allowing the bot to greet users in their preferred language.

## Geeky and Nerdy References

The bot will incorporate geeky and nerdy references in its greetings, making the interaction more engaging for users who appreciate pop culture, technology, and science fiction.

### References Examples

- "Greetings, fellow traveler of the multiverse!"
- "Hello, young Padawan! May the Force be with you."
- "Hi there! Ready to embark on a quest for knowledge?"

### Themes

- Science Fiction
- Programming and Technology
- Gaming and Fantasy
