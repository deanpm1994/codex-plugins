# DPI-Design

Work through the design phase one decision at a time until the shape of the project is clear.

Ask questions one at a time and wait for the response before moving on.
If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, do that instead.

When a choice is needed, show at least two options, explain the trade-offs, and lead with your recommendation.
